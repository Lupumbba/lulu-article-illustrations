# Cartea Falcon Robot IP

## Visual Identity

The Cartea falcon is a white robotic falcon character. It must preserve the exact external design language shown in the bundled reference images:

- `assets/reference/front.png`
- `assets/reference/back.png`
- `assets/reference/side.png`
- `assets/reference/side-alt.png`

Treat these images as identity-lock references, not loose style inspiration.

Mandatory external features:

- White glossy mechanical shell.
- Black glass visor across the face.
- Green illuminated UI/camera rings on the visor.
- Angular white falcon beak integrated into the head.
- Black textured neck area.
- White chest armor with green glowing panels.
- Wing-like mechanical arms with layered mint-green feathers/panels.
- Black articulated hands.
- Wheel-based legs/feet.
- Chibi mechanical proportions: large head, compact torso, short powerful legs.
- Green accent color must use Cartea green `#00a600` wherever branding or glow is needed.

The falcon must perform the core action in the image:

- scanning the market with visor sensors
- pointing with a wing
- balancing city and desert routes
- guiding SUVs through a route map
- stamping credibility
- guarding a service network
- sorting models into a product ladder

## Personality

- Decisive, observant, quick, and reliable.
- More like a robotic market analyst and desert mobility guide than a cute mascot.
- Calm confidence, not aggressive.
- Premium enough for automotive strategy content.

## Avoid

- Do not redesign the character silhouette.
- Do not change the head, visor, beak, wings, wheel-feet, or body proportions.
- Do not turn it into a natural feathered falcon, eagle, owl, phoenix, or generic bird.
- Do not make it plush-like, sticker-like, or a different robot.
- Do not replace Cartea green `#00a600` with orange, blue, purple, or random neon colors.
- Do not use an official Cartea logo unless the user provides it.
- Do not place it as a tiny corner decoration.
