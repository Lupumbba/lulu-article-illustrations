# QA Checklist

Before delivery, verify:

- The character matches the bundled robot falcon references: white shell, black visor, green visor rings, white beak, wing-like mechanical arms, wheel-feet.
- The character was not redesigned, stretched, deformed, or turned into a natural bird.
- Cartea green `#00a600` is the dominant brand/highlight color.
- Orange is not used as the Cartea brand color.
- The falcon robot performs the core action.
- The image is readable at article width.
- The style is premium editorial hand-drawn, not PPT or ad poster.
- Labels are short and legible.
- The image matches the article point, not just the article topic.
- No official logo is imitated.
- Final file dimensions match the requested width and height.
- File names are ordered and descriptive.

Regenerate or edit if:

- The falcon robot is decorative.
- The falcon robot's exterior no longer matches the reference images.
- Cartea marks, lines, UI glow, or labels use the wrong brand color.
- The image is too crowded.
- Cars dominate the image so strongly that the IP disappears.
- The result looks like a dealership ad instead of an article illustration.
- Text is mostly unreadable or misspelled.
- The output size is wrong after normalization.
