# Automotive Article Shot List

## What To Extract

From a car article, look for:

- Product ladder: entry model, family model, premium model, off-road model.
- Usage split: city, highway, family, desert, mountain, touring.
- Proof points: horsepower, torque, drivetrain, seats, price, service network.
- Market conflict: challenger brand versus established brands.
- Trust layer: dealer group, workshops, parts center, warranty, long-term support.

## Best Visual Structures

- Route map: city road becomes desert trail.
- Product ladder: models climb from accessible SUV to premium off-road machine.
- Capability compass: comfort, cooling, space, 4WD, off-road credibility.
- Falcon lookout: the falcon spots market gaps from above.
- Service network: showroom, workshop, parts hub connected like a safe landing route.
- Brand challenge arena: challenger entering a field dominated by older brands.

## Shot List Template

Use this concise format:

```text
1. [title]
   Placement: after [section]
   Core idea: [one sentence]
   Falcon action: [specific action]
   Elements: [short list]
   Labels: [short labels]
   Size: [width]x[height]
```
